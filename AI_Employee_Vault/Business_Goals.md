# Business Goals & KPIs

## Revenue Targets
- **Monthly Revenue Target:** $10,000
- **Quarterly Revenue Target:** $30,000
- **Annual Revenue Target:** $120,000

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Financial
- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- Accounts Receivable (outstanding invoices)
- Payment collection rate (% invoices paid on time)
- Operating expenses vs revenue ratio

### Client Management
- Active clients count
- New clients acquired this month
- Client churn rate
- Average response time to client inquiries

### Productivity
- Tasks completed per week
- Average task completion time
- Items pending approval (backlog)
- Error/retry rate

### Social Media
- Posts published per platform per week
- Engagement rate (likes, comments, shares)
- Follower growth rate

## Subscription Audit Rules
- Review all active subscriptions monthly
- Flag subscriptions with no usage in 30+ days
- Flag subscriptions exceeding $50/month
- Ensure no duplicate subscriptions across platforms
- Track total subscription spend vs budget ($500/month cap)

## Briefing Schedule
- **Monday Morning Briefing:** Weekly summary generated every Monday
- **Monthly Report:** First Monday of each month includes monthly rollup
- **Quarterly Review:** First Monday of quarter includes quarterly analysis

## Alert Thresholds
- Revenue below 80% of monthly target: **HIGH ALERT**
- Outstanding invoices > 30 days: **FOLLOW UP**
- Task backlog > 10 items: **CAPACITY WARNING**
- Social media: no posts in 7+ days: **ENGAGEMENT WARNING**
